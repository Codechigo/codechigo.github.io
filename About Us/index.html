About Us

A Nigerian company, with headquarters located in Lagos. We provide Value Added Services to Nigerian Telecom operators; lifestyle & business applications and services to our customers. We are Licensed by Nigerian Communications Commission and started full Operations as a VAS and Mobile Application Development company.

Xenolith Mobile provides SMS Subscription services, CRBT services, Ring Tunes, Mobile Application Services, Video on Demand services and Customized Applications for Nigerian OEMs. Our mission is to create value for our partners and at the same time add value to the daily lives of our customers.

Vision Statement

To be the innovative and leading Value Added Services and Mobile Application development company in sub-Saharan Africa.

Mission Statement

To provide our customers with quality and relevant content that will add value to their daily lives and grow their businesses.

Values
